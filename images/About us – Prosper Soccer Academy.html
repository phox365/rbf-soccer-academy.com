Brief information about Prosper Soccer Academy

The roots of Prosper Soccer Academy traces back in 2010 when it was founded with the intention of identifying talents in many young children who did not have a chance to get formal Soccer training. It became  necessary to start this academy that would train and spot out talented players who would thereafter be uplifted to become professional players.

We have taught young players how to play the game and conduct themselves as professionals. We have  as well mentored and groomed many who are now participating in many  Clubs around the world and we continue to do so for better future of the game & soccer stars who pass through our hands.

We are nurturing youngsters in soccer fraternity from the age of 3 years up to the under18 stage, 

We continue to participate in national junior leagues  organized under FUFA guidelines

 Our under 17 team  always participate in FUFA Junior’s league  (which is organized annually) and junior’s local and international football galas as well as friendly matches.

 Our consistent   support towards soccer, integrity and  professional technical team,   has seen us register more students year in year out.

Prosper Soccer Academy continues to produce well trained soccer professionals who conduct themselves with discipline, integrity and passion in the pitch and in society.
We connect our students with professional coaches and players who provide extra knowledge and link them to diverse World of Soccer

Our Mission

To groom potential football talents with the most excellent proficiency of the game through providing a rounded education which instill understanding of the values of winning and the discipline that is essential for them to reach their pinnacle of excellency.

Our Vision

To be the leading Soccer Academy in East Africa

Player's Pathway and Empowerment of Prosper Soccer Academy

We teach young soccer players the mental and moral obligations that come with the game of football. 
Our goal is to send them onto the other greater Soccer Opportunities around the world.

.Preparing Players for a Professional Soccer career

 

    1. We don’t impose limitation from coaches on what players can do and as such allow them to have creativity in training which is a great asset when they become professionals.

    1. We encourage players to start their own games and assess each other’s performance, discussing solutions at the same time. This continues to greatly improve their leadership skills which is a wonderful attributes to have as a professionals.

    1. We encourage players to assess whether something they have tried has worked or not. Individual player assessment is one of the most important attributes when it comes to being professional

    1. We encourage players to set goals. Studies has proved that setting goals can boost their  performance on the pitch. With such a long and difficult journey to making it as a pro, they need to a way of regularly checking their progress. This is where goal setting comes in handy.

We Prepare Players for Life beyond football

 

    1. Guided by our trained coach/mentors, players learn teamwork and develop leadership skills that  help them become productive citizen.

    1. Our proven training program teaches /coaches how to build soccer skills, and just as importantly as to build character and life skills through play.

    1. We teach youth from all parts of the communities the fundamentals of soccer while striving to help them build confidence and recognize the value of hard work, teamwork and persistence in achieving personal goals.lent

 

Our Academy Coaching and Soccer Philosophy.

The academy philosophy encompasses a number of areas. It outlines our aims on the pitch, Values, styles of the soccer , culture, our coaching methods and also the standards we expect from all associated with the club be that parents, players or coaches.
